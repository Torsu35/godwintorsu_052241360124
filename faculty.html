Academics History

The Faculty of Applied Sciences and Technology was carved out from the then Faculty of Applied Sciences in October, 2016. The reason for renaming the Faculty was to help introduce new technology-related programmes and also to properly manage the existing ones in the institution.

Welcome To Our Faculty

Greetings! Welcome to the Faculty of Applied Science and Technology. Thanks for visiting our Faculty.

Mission

Our faculty is committed to fostering academic excellence, promoting innovation, and nurturing a culture of inclusivity and collaboration. We strive to empower our students with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and ethical values to become leaders in their respective fields and contribute positively to society.

Vision

Our vision is to be a dynamic and forward-thinking academic community dedicated to advancing knowledge through teaching, research, and service. We aim to cultivate a supportive environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, creativity, and interdisciplinary collaboration. By embracing diversity and embracing emerging technologies, we aspire to be at the forefront of education and make a meaningful impact on local and global challenges.

Our Philosphy

At the core of our philosophy lies a commitment to excellence, inclusivity, and empowerment, driving us to create a dynamic learning environment where every individual thrives.

Academics Principle

Principle: Upholding academic integrity, promoting critical thinking, and fostering a passion for lifelong learning form the foundational principles that guide our academic pursuits.

Key of Success

Consistent effort, resilience in the face of challenges, and a growth mindset are the key ingredients that pave the way for success in our journey.
